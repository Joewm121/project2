Backround

After high school I went to work for a Beer distributor, I started off as Merchandiser where I was tasked with filling the shelves of local stores, after a few years I was promoted to a unique position within Customer Service, I was to become the "jack of all trades" for the department, as well as being the delivery driver for orders that were missed or forgotten. After a couple years, many attempts to get into Sales and a break up, I was tired, burnt out and need a change. For me 2016 was spent grinding through my work days and selling most what I owned in preparation for what I love most;Traveling. Australia provided me time not only to explore an amazing country but to also let me think and make rational decisions on what my next move should be. I spent 4 months backpacking around the country experiencing all I could. In the end had some amazing experiences, from 4,000km road trips to working on a dive boat and getting to dive 3 times a day. I met tons of amazing people from all walks of life, each one seeking something different with a different outlook on life.

Aardvarkian Paradise

  • Add classes to style large chunks of our page.
  • applying classes to multiple HTML tags.
  • inking an email address with the anchor tag.
  • Languages: HTML,CSS, JavaScript.

Vacation Page

In this example we were practicing using:

  • A container to pad all content.
  • A Jumbotron to hold a title, and an introductory sentence or slogan.
  • a section with blockquotes to contain what previous visitors to this area have said.
  • several helper classes to add color that emphasizes certain areas of the page.
  • Languages: HTML,CSS.
